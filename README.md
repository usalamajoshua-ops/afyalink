<h1>🌍 AfyaLink</h1>
<p><strong>Confidential support. Real help. When it matters most.</strong></p>

<div class="section box">
<h2>📌 Overview</h2>
<p>
AfyaLink is an anonymous youth health platform designed to provide safe, accessible, and real-time support for young people in Rwanda.
It focuses on sexual and reproductive health, mental health, abuse reporting, and youth education.
</p>
</div>

<div class="section box">
<h2>🚀 Key Features</h2>
<ul>
  <li><strong>Anonymous Chat:</strong> Safe space to ask sensitive questions</li>
  <li><strong>Emergency Support:</strong> Direct calls to Isange (3512) and Police (116)</li>
  <li><strong>Quick Learning:</strong> Myth vs fact education</li>
  <li><strong>Find Help:</strong> Access nearby health services</li>
  <li><strong>Government Dashboard:</strong> Secure insights for RIB and RNP</li>
</ul>
</div>

<div class="section box">
<h2>🧠 Innovation</h2>
<p>
AfyaLink acts as a digital safe space, early warning system, and decision-support tool for public health institutions.
</p>

<h2>🎯 Problem</h2>
<p>
Many youth face health challenges and abuse that remain unreported due to stigma, fear, and lack of confidential support.
</p>
</div>

<div class="section box">
<h2>💡 Solution</h2>
<p>
AfyaLink provides anonymous communication, emergency access, and real-time insights without compromising identity.
</p>
</div>

<div class="section box">
<h2>🔒 Privacy</h2>
<p>
No personal data required. All insights are aggregated and anonymous. Access to sensitive data is restricted.
</p>
</div>

<div class="section box">
<h2>🏆 Vision</h2>
<p>
To scale AfyaLink into a national platform that improves youth health and strengthens institutional response systems.
